Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and respect your privacy

Enhanced Privacy with Client-Side Processing

All file conversions and batch processing (up to 100 files) happen entirely in your browser. Your documents and images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At ImageResizer Pro, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website and tools.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. No Image Storage

We want to be absolutely clear: We do not store, upload, or save your images on our servers. All image processing happens locally in your browser.

1.2. Anonymous Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our services:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)
  • Country/region (based on IP address)
  • Features used and duration of use
  • Error reports to improve stability

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users.

1.3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser storage to:

  • Remember your preferences (language, theme)
  • Store your custom presets locally
  • Maintain session information

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

2. How We Process Images

Our image processing operates entirely client-side:

  • Images never leave your device
  • No server uploads required
  • Processing happens in your browser's memory
  • Files are deleted from memory after processing

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy)
  • Font Awesome: For icons (privacy policy: Font Awesome Privacy Policy)
  • Tesseract.js: For OCR text extraction (runs locally)
  • jsQR: For QR code scanning (runs locally)

4. Contact Forms

When you use our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Message content

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • No sensitive data storage

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Export your data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@imageresizerpro.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Us

10. Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the United States.

Note: This is a simplified privacy policy for demonstration purposes. In a real application, you should consult with a legal professional to ensure compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.
