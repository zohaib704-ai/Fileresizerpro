Frequently Asked Questions

Is ImageResizer Pro really free?

Yes, ImageResizer Pro is completely free to use! We don't charge any fees, require registration, or place watermarks on your images. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device and are not uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security for your files.

What image formats do you support?

We support all major image formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WEBP. You can convert between these formats during the compression process.

Can I use ImageResizer Pro on mobile?

Yes! Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, we limit individual files to 10MB for optimal performance. Most smartphone photos are 2-5MB, so this covers the vast majority of use cases.

Can businesses use ImageResizer Pro?

Absolutely! Many businesses use our tools for website optimization, product images, marketing materials, and social media content. We also offer enterprise solutions for larger needs.
